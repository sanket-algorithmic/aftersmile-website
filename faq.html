AfterSmile FAQ

Everything you need to know about building a portfolio that grows your practice

What is AfterSmile? +

AfterSmile is a professional dental portfolio app designed to help you capture, organize, and showcase your smile transformations. Think of it as your personal marketing tool that turns your clinical excellence into compelling visual stories that attract premium patients and grow your practice.

How does AfterSmile help me grow my practice? +

Your best work is your most powerful marketing asset. AfterSmile helps you document your smile transformations with professional quality, organize them by procedure type, and share them effortlessly during consultations or on social media. When prospects see what you've achieved for others, they envision what you can do for them - that's when consultations convert into treatments.

How much does AfterSmile cost? +

AfterSmile is free to download and use. All features including photo capture, portfolio organization, and before-and-after comparison tools are included at no cost. Your investment is simply your time to build a portfolio that can generate significant returns through increased case acceptance.

What makes a great dental portfolio? +

A great portfolio tells transformation stories. It showcases your range - from simple whitening to complex full-mouth reconstructions. The key is quality over quantity: select your most dramatic transformations, ensure consistent lighting and angles, and organize by procedure type so you can quickly show relevant cases to each prospect.

How do I take better before and after photos? +

AfterSmile makes it simple: use consistent angles (front smile, retracted views, close-ups), maintain good lighting, and capture both before and after with the same framing. Our built-in comparison tool helps you align photos perfectly, creating that powerful visual impact that demonstrates your skill and attention to detail.

Where are my portfolio photos stored? +

All images are stored locally on your device, giving you complete control. Your portfolio stays with you, accessible anytime without requiring an internet connection. This ensures you can showcase your work during consultations even in areas with limited connectivity.

Can I share my portfolio with patients? +

Absolutely. AfterSmile makes it effortless to share individual cases or entire procedure categories. Show prospects during consultations, share success stories on social media, or send specific examples to help patients envision their own transformation. The easier it is to share, the more your work speaks for itself.

How do I organize my cases effectively? +

AfterSmile lets you organize by procedure type, complexity, or any system that works for your practice. Create folders for veneers, implants, orthodontics, whitening - whatever categories help you quickly find the perfect case to show each prospect. A well-organized portfolio saves time and increases your credibility.

Do I need patient consent to photograph their treatment? +

Yes, always obtain written consent before photographing and especially before sharing patient images. AfterSmile provides the tools to capture and organize your work; you're responsible for ensuring proper consent and compliance with applicable regulations. Most patients are proud to be part of your success stories when asked appropriately.

What devices does AfterSmile support? +

AfterSmile works on both iOS (15.5+) and Android (7.0+) devices, including smartphones and tablets. Whether you prefer iPhone or Android, you can build and showcase your portfolio with professional quality on the device you already carry.

How quickly can I start building my portfolio? +

Immediately. Download AfterSmile, capture your first before photo, and you've started. As you complete treatments, add the after photos. Within weeks, you'll have a curated collection of transformations. The dentists who see the fastest practice growth are those who consistently document their best work.

Can I use AfterSmile for social media marketing? +

Definitely. Social media is one of the most powerful ways to showcase your artistry. Export polished before-and-after comparisons directly from AfterSmile to Instagram, Facebook, or your website. Consistent sharing of great results builds your reputation and attracts patients actively seeking transformation.

What if I'm not tech-savvy? +

AfterSmile is designed to be intuitive. If you can take a photo with your phone, you can use AfterSmile. The interface is clean and straightforward - capture, organize, compare, share. No complicated settings or technical knowledge required. Your focus should be on the dentistry; we handle the technology.

How do I get support or suggest features? +

We're here to help you succeed. Email us at info@algorithmic.co with questions, feedback, or feature requests. We actively listen to our community of dentists and continuously improve AfterSmile based on what helps you grow your practice most effectively.

Why is showcasing my work so important? +

People buy based on results they can see. When you show a prospect a transformation similar to what they want, you bypass skepticism and build instant credibility. Your portfolio answers the unspoken question every patient has: "Can you really help me?" A strong portfolio turns that doubt into confidence, and confidence into commitment.
